About Anmol

I'm a trauma-informed and queer-affirmative therapist with over six years of experience supporting adults through challenges related to anxiety, depression, trauma, addictions, eating disorders, and relationship issues to name a few.

My work is rooted in compassion and evidence-based practices. I integrate approaches like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Dialectical Behavioural Therapy (DBT) and Nervous system based work to help clients move toward meaningful change at a pace that feels safe and sustainable.

I take a mind-body approach to healing, often helping clients understand how their nervous system responds to stress, burnout, and emotional overwhelm. Together there is collaborative work on expanding their window of tolerance, rebuilding self-trust, and creating regulation practices that support long-term emotional balance.

In my private practice, I've had the privilege of working with clients from around the world, offering a safe, affirming, and non-judgmental space where they can explore their experiences and reconnect with themselves.

My aim is not only to help with managing symptoms but to help build a life that feels authentic, grounded, and aligned with who they truly are.
